Jango

Info Clones Slave 1

Jango Fett is the "Father" of Jango Fett. Count Dooku requested Jango's DNA in order to make the clone army and in exchange Jango got his own clone to raise as his son. The "son" is Boba Fett. When Jango died in Star Wars episode 2 his armour and ship became Boba's.
